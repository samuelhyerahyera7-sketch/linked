Terms & Privacy

Last updated June 2026

1. Who we are

Linked is a social connection platform designed for people in South Africa to meet, date, and make friends. By using the app you agree to these terms.

2. Eligibility

You must be 18 or older to use Linked. By creating a profile you confirm you meet this requirement.

3. Your profile

Your profile must be accurate and represent you. Fake profiles, impersonation, or misleading content is strictly prohibited and will result in removal.

4. Respectful behaviour

Harassment, hate speech, explicit unsolicited content, or any abusive behaviour is not tolerated. We reserve the right to suspend or permanently ban accounts that violate this policy.

5. Privacy & data

We collect only the information needed to run the service — your name, age, photos, and location city. We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Your phone number is used only for authentication and is never visible to other users.

6. Photos & content

By uploading photos you grant Linked a licence to display them within the app. You retain ownership. Do not upload content that belongs to someone else or that contains nudity, violence, or illegal material.

7. Payments

Premium subscriptions are billed in ZAR and renew automatically. You may cancel at any time. Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis in line with South African Consumer Protection Act guidelines.

8. Contact

Questions? Email us at hello@linked.co.za
